About Sparks Consulting Group

A data-focused advisory firm dedicated to helping small business owners gain clarity and make smarter decisions.

Our Mission

Sparks Consulting Group helps small business owners gain clarity from their data and make smarter, faster, and more confident decisions. We believe data should be practical, understandable, and directly connected to real business outcomes.

Meet Dyllan Sparks

Founder & Managing Partner

Dyllan founded Sparks Consulting Group to help small and growing businesses make sense of their financial and operational data and turn it into actionable insights. He brings technical expertise, business strategy knowledge, and a practical mindset to every engagement.

Dyllan specializes in organizing complex information, identifying meaningful patterns, and translating data into clear, practical guidance. His approach emphasizes structure, clarity, and results, ensuring that data empowers decisions rather than complicates them.
