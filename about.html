About Sparks Consulting Group

Sparks Consulting Group is a data-focused advisory firm that helps small business owners gain clarity from their numbers and make smarter, faster, and more confident decisions.

We believe data should be practical, understandable, and directly connected to real business outcomes. We avoid overwhelming dashboards or reports that go unused. Our work is centered on turning raw information into clear insights that support smarter strategy and day-to-day decision-making.

Founder & Managing Partner

Dyllan Sparks

Sparks Consulting Group was founded by Dyllan Sparks to help small and growing businesses make sense of their financial and operational data and turn it into actionable insights.

Dyllan holds a Bachelor's degree in Marketing from the University of Northern Iowa and an MBA from Upper Iowa University, providing a strong foundation in business strategy and analytics. He is currently completing his Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) at Drake University, further strengthening his expertise in data-driven decision-making.

He specializes in organizing complex information, identifying meaningful patterns, and translating data into clear, practical guidance. His approach emphasizes structure, clarity, and results, ensuring that data empowers decisions rather than complicates them.

Dyllan works closely with business owners to understand their goals and challenges, delivering solutions tailored to each business's unique needs. He lives in Waterloo, Iowa with his wife and son.

Our Approach

We take a straightforward, collaborative approach to analytics and advisory work.

Rather than applying generic templates or overly technical models, we focus on:

  • Understanding the business first
  • Simplifying and structuring existing data
  • Highlighting what matters most
  • Delivering insights that can be acted on immediately

Our goal is not to overwhelm clients with information, but to provide clarity and direction through well-organized data and thoughtful analysis.

Who We Work With

Sparks Consulting Group primarily works with:

  • Small business owners and founders
  • Owner-operated and founder-led companies
  • Businesses seeking better visibility into their financial or operational data
  • Teams that want clear insights without unnecessary complexity

We are best suited for organizations that value clarity, transparency, and practical decision-making.

Why Clients Choose Sparks Consulting Group

Clients choose Sparks Consulting Group for:

  • Clear, structured analysis that's easy to understand
  • A practical, business-first mindset
  • Direct communication without jargon
  • Solutions designed to support real decisions, not just reporting

We view our role as a trusted partner, focused on helping clients move forward with confidence.
