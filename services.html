Consulting Services

Practical data analysis and decision support for small business owners who want clarity, not complexity.

How We Support Your Business

Sparks Consulting Group partners with small businesses to make data usable, understandable, and actionable. Our focus is on helping you see what matters, reduce manual effort, and make informed decisions with confidence.

Common Challenges We Address

  • Spreadsheets that are messy, inconsistent, or difficult to use
  • Large amounts of data without clear insights
  • Manual reporting that consumes valuable time
  • Uncertainty around trends, performance, or risk
  • Difficulty planning or forecasting future performance

Services Offered

  • Data Cleanup & Organization: Structuring spreadsheets so your data is accurate, consistent, and analysis-ready.
  • Insight & Trend Analysis: Identifying patterns, opportunities, and risks hidden in your data.
  • Dashboards & Visuals: Creating simple charts and dashboards for quick, at-a-glance understanding.
  • Automation & Efficiency: Reducing manual work through formulas, models, and streamlined reporting.
  • Planning & Forecasting: Building projections to support budgeting and strategic decisions.

What You Can Expect

  • Clean, structured spreadsheets ready for ongoing use
  • Clear identification of key metrics and trends
  • Simple, easy-to-understand visuals and summaries
  • Actionable recommendations and next steps
  • Support and explanation throughout the process
