What We Do

At Sparks Consulting Group, we help small business owners unlock the value in their data, enabling smarter, faster, and more informed decisions.

The Challenges We Solve

  • Disorganized or messy data: We clean, organize, and structure your spreadsheets to make them actionable.
  • Identifying patterns and trends: We highlight key insights so you can spot opportunities and risks quickly.
  • Turning numbers into decisions: We create clear visuals and summaries that tell the story behind your data.
  • Time-consuming manual work: We automate calculations and reporting in Excel or Google Sheets.
  • Planning and forecasting: We build models and projections to guide business strategy.

How We Deliver Value

  • Cleaned and structured spreadsheets ready for analysis
  • Highlighted key metrics and trends
  • Simple dashboards and visuals for “at-a-glance” insights
  • Actionable summaries with clear recommendations
